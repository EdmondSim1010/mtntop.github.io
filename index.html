Tired of Predictable Success?

At Mountain Top Education, we believe that true growth comes from radical uncertainty. Why settle for a guaranteed 99.95 ATAR when you could have a life story? We're the only educational institution brave enough to ask, "What if we just... winged it?"

Meet the High-Rollers: Our ATAR Roulette Tutors

Will you get a tutor who topped the state in 8 subjects, or one who spells their own name with a charming degree of creative flair? Place your bets! Four of these paragons of academia have 99.5+ ATARs, numerous state ranks, and have probably forgotten more about success than you'll ever know. One of them... has a different kind of life experience. An ATAR below 30, to be precise. It's a character-building lottery!

Dr. Araminta 'State Rank' Worthington

With a 99.95 ATAR and more state ranks than she has houseplants, Araminta is a finely tuned academic machine. She communicates exclusively in syllabi and has never experienced a moment of self-doubt. Ideal for those who find inspiration in unrelenting perfection.

Bartholomew 'The Prodigy' Chen

Achieved a 99.85 ATAR while simultaneously composing a three-part symphony for oboe and theremin. Bartholomew believes sleep is a social construct and that free time is a myth created by the underachievers. His passion is both inspiring and deeply unsettling.

Genevieve '99.5 is basically failing' Dubois

Genevieve views her 99.5 ATAR as a minor but embarrassing setback. She has an encyclopedic knowledge of everything and will ensure you understand not just the subject, but also the profound disappointment of not being first. Prepare to be elevated and slightly broken.

Xavier 'The Benchmark' Vandelay

Xavier is our fourth 99.5+ prodigy. He doesn't just teach; he becomes the living embodiment of academic rigor. His past students have described him as 'intense,' 'relentless,' and 'probably a cyborg.' Guaranteed results, questionable methods.

Specialist Tutor: Barry

Then there's Barry. His ATAR is... under 30. Why is he here? We believe in the educational power of contrast. Barry will teach you resilience, the importance of low expectations, and how to find joy in simple things, like successfully turning on a computer. He's a walking, talking lesson in 'it could be worse'.

Our Bespoke Subject Offerings

Juggling

Metaphorically and literally. Learn to juggle your academic workload, social life, and flaming torches (waiver required).

Gambling

The core of our educational model. Master probability, risk assessment, and the art of knowing when to walk away (from your exams).

Clowning

Develop essential life skills such as fitting into small spaces and making people uncomfortable with your silence. Red nose optional, existential dread guaranteed.

Curling

Embrace the hypnotic monotony of sliding rocks on ice. It's the perfect preparation for a career in bureaucracy.

Chimpanzees

An advanced seminar in primate politics and social hierarchy. Learn to navigate complex social structures by observing our closest living relatives. Flirtation with a chimp is strictly prohibited.

Stress Eating

From nervous nibbling to full-scale panic-binging, our course covers the full spectrum of culinary coping mechanisms. BYO snacks.

Maximising Mental Illness

Turn that nagging sense of unease into a full-blown, avant-garde personality. We'll help you weaponize your anxiety and monetize your neuroses.

Being Silly

An intensive workshop for those who take life too seriously. We will systematically dismantle your dignity until only pure, unadulterated goofiness remains.

Comedy

The logical follow-up to 'Maximising Mental Illness.' Learn to turn your trauma into tight five-minute sets. Laughter is the best medicine, after all.

Performativity

Explore the works of Judith Butler while learning how to convincingly fake a personality. Perfect for job interviews and first dates.

Composition with Joshua Lampour

The enigmatic Joshua Lampour, a visionary whose work is "pre-nostalgic," offers lessons in composing music that has never been heard and, some say, should never be. An acquired taste.

Chinglish (Adv, Ext 1, Ext 2)

A comprehensive suite of courses on the beautiful, chaotic fusion of Chinese and English. Move beyond "long time no see" to advanced poetic and philosophical concepts. For the discerning linguistic adventurer.

Gym

We have a room with some heavy things in it. You can pick them up and put them down. We're not sure what else happens in there. It smells of effort.
